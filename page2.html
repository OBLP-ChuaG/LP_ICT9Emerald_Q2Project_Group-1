Courses Offer

CASA

The Casa Program encourages the transformation of children from ages 3 to 5 as they consciously and willingly learn from their environment. Students develop their personalities, will, and intelligence through exploration and discovery with Montessori materials.

PRIMARY

In the Primary Grade School Program, the prepared environment supports the intellectual and physical development of students ages 6 to 8 until they become confident and independent.

INTERMEDIATE

Students ages 9 to 12 deepen their knowledge about universal responsibility to care for life and the environment. The curriculum enhances academic performance while encouraging teamwork, leadership, and problem-solving.

JUNIOR HIGHSCHOOL

The Junior High School Program supports the emotional, psychological, and social growth of students ages 13 to 16 as they prepare for adult responsibilities. It strengthens collaboration, communication, and critical thinking.

SENIOR HIGHSCHOOL

The Senior High School Program equips students ages 17 and 18 with the knowledge and skills needed for future success. The GAS Plus curriculum provides flexibility in exploring career paths and preparing for college or university.
