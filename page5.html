Who We Are

We are the first group (GROUP 1) in 9E of the ICT Project of QUARTER 2. Our plan and focus was to structure and create a website for our school, OB Montessori Center, not remotely similar but somehow the same. We tried to implement the same interactions and parts of the website without making it look exactly identical to it. We also followed the guidelines as a guide to help us with our website.
